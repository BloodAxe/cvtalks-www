About Me

April 09, 2014

Hi, an welcome to Computer Vision Talks blog!

Years ago, in 2010 I’ve started writing about computer vision.

A lot of things happened since that. I’ve participated in many exciting projects, wrote book, grew new skills and found many new friends. This page is not a CV, but rather a hall of fame with a list of buzzwords describing techs I’ve worked with:

  • Monocular SLAM
  • 3D scene reconstruction from point cloud
  • Object recognition
  • 3D Visualization
  • Marker-less and marker-based augmented reality
  • Computer vision and image processing
  • Real-time media processing

Hope you enjoy reading my blog.
